# adidas-dashboard

### Introduction

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analysing Adidas sales performance across different products, retailers, regions, and sales channels. The goal of the dashboard is to identify key drivers of revenue, profitability, and product demand by exploring trends in sales, operating profit, and units sold. By visualising these patterns, the dashboard provides insights that can help businesses understand which products, markets, and retailers contribute most to overall performance.


### Dataset Description

The dataset contains Adidas sales transactions across multiple regions in the United States. Key variables include:

* **Sales revenue**
* **Units sold**
* **Operating profit**
* **Product category**
* **Retailer**
* **Region and state**
* **Sales method** (online, in-store, outlet)
* **Time period** (2019–2021)

These variables allow analysis of both product-level and regional sales performance.


### Dashboard Features

The Tableau dashboard includes several components designed to highlight different aspects of Adidas sales performance:

* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)** showing total sales, total units sold, operating profit, average price per unit, and number of retailers.
* **Product performance analysis** displaying average operating margin and units sold by product category.
* **Retailer comparison** showing total sales generated by different retail partners.
* **Regional performance analysis** highlighting operating profit and sales across geographic regions.
* **Sales channel breakdown** comparing revenue generated from in-store, online, and outlet sales.
* **Time trend analysis** showing how sales evolved between 2019 and 2021.
* **State-level sales distribution** visualised using a treemap.


### Key Insights

Several insights can be observed from the dashboard:

* **Men’s Street Footwear records the highest unit sales**, indicating strong demand within this product category.
* **West Gear and Foot Locker generate the largest sales revenue among retailers**, suggesting strong retail partnerships.
* **The West region produces the highest operating profit**, highlighting it as the most profitable market.
* **In-store sales account for the largest share of revenue**, outperforming both online and outlet channels.
* **Sales show strong growth from 2020 to 2021**, indicating increasing demand or recovery in the retail market.

These insights demonstrate how sales performance varies across products, retailers, and geographic markets.


### Tools Used

* **Tableau** – dashboard creation and visualisation
* **Data visualisation techniques** – KPI cards, bar charts, pie charts, treemaps, and time series analysis
